Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Study Stream.

Do I need to create an account to use Study Stream?

No, you do not need to create an account to use Study Stream. Simply visit the website and start using the timer and task features.

What should I do if I forget my password?

As you do not need an account to use Study Stream, there's no need to worry about passwords.

How can I use the timer technique on Study Stream?

Navigate to the 'Timer' section, set your desired study duration, and start the timer to begin your focused study session.

How do I add tasks to my study plan?

Go to the 'Tasks' section, click on 'Add Task', enter the details of your task, and save it. You can manage and track your tasks from this section.

Can I track my study progress on Study Stream?

Yes, Study Stream offers a progress tracking feature where you can view your study statistics, completed tasks, and overall productivity.

Is there any mobile app available for Study Stream?

Yes, you can download our app from the App Store or Google Play Store to access Study Stream on the go.

Troubleshoot

If you're experiencing issues with the Study Stream mobile app, find solutions below.

The timer is not working. What should I do?

Ensure that your app is up to date by checking for updates in the App Store or Google Play Store. If the issue persists, try closing and reopening the app or restarting your device.

I can't add tasks. What might be the issue?

Check if the app has the necessary permissions to access your device's storage. You can do this in your device's settings. If you are still unable to add tasks, try reinstalling the app.

The app is running slowly. What can I do?

Close any other apps running in the background to free up resources. Ensure your device has sufficient storage space and try restarting your device.

I'm encountering an error message. How do I resolve it?

Take a screenshot of the error message and check our 'Help Center' for specific troubleshooting steps. If the issue persists, contact our support team with the details of the error.

How can I report a bug or technical issue?

You can report bugs or technical issues via the 'Contact Us' section within the app or by emailing us at skmdizaz1@gmail.com. Please provide as much detail as possible about the issue.

The app isn't displaying correctly on my device. What should I do?

Ensure that your device's operating system is up to date. Try clearing the app's cache or reinstalling the app. If the problem continues, contact our support team for further assistance.
